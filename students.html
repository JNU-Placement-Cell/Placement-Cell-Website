Rules, guidelines, and resources for JNU students participating in campus placements.

Rules and Guidelines for Students

  • Students should regularly visit the JNU Placement Cell website for jobs and internships updates.
  • Interested students must register with the Placement Cell and upload their CV or resume by filling the Student Registration Form (see below).
  • Please refer to student placement policy for more information.

Frequently Asked Questions

How will I come to know about job vacancies?

The JNU Placement Cell posts job/internships/events announcements through official channels.

How many organisations can I apply for?

Please refer to the student placement policy for more information.

Are there any school-specific organisations invited?

Yes, both school-specific and multi-disciplinary organizations are invited to conduct placement drives.

How do organisations shortlist programs they wish to interact with?

Organisations shortlist students as per their policy.

Is it mandatory to participate in the campus placement?

No, it is not mandatory. It is completely the choice of the students whether to participate in campus placements or not. .

is it mandatory to follow resume format?

No. It is optional for students to follow the resume format.
