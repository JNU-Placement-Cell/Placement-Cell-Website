Placement Procedure

1

The Placement Cell shall send invitations to recruiting organizations for campus internship and placement processes. Interested organizations may also send us an email at placement@jnu.ac.in

2

Organizations may register with us by filling out Job Notification Form (JNF) for internships as well as placements

3

Once the NF is filled, organizations are contacted for further processes.

4

Organizations may conduct pre-processes and for which they can request with relevant details.

After selection, the company will announce the names of the selected candidates at the end of the process.
