About Jawaharlal Nehru University

Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), established in 1969, is one of India's premier public universities, consistently ranked among the top institutions in the country. Located in New Delhi, JNU is renowned for its academic excellence, vibrant intellectual culture, and strong commitment to social responsibility.

The university comprises 13 schools and 8 special centres, offering a wide array of interdisciplinary programs across the engineering, management, languages, humanities, social sciences and sciences. JNU fosters critical thinking, innovation, and inclusive learning through its research-intensive curriculum and close faculty-student engagement.

Accredited with NAAC A++ and ranked 2nd in NIRF 2025 (overall university category), JNU is home to a diverse and dynamic student community mentored by some of the country's most distinguished faculty. Its alumni have made significant contributions across academia, civil services, media, politics, national and international organizations, and the corporate world.

JNU graduates bring with them a unique blend of analytical depth, ethical awareness, and global perspective. Whether you're seeking engineers, managers, researchers, policy experts, consultants, data scientists, economists, language experts or any other professionals, JNU students are equipped to deliver meaningful impact in today's evolving professional landscape.

About Placement Cell

The Placement Cell at Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) serves as a vital link between the academic world and the professional sphere. It is committed to facilitating meaningful career opportunities for students by connecting them with a wide range of national and international organizations across sectors.

The Cell works proactively to engage with recruiters, organize campus placements, internships and support students in their career aspirations. It also conducts skill development workshops, career counseling sessions, resume-building guidance, and interview preparation activities to ensure students are industry-ready.

With a focus on both employment and internships, the Placement Cell caters to students from diverse academic backgrounds, including engineering, management, social sciences, sciences, language studies, international relations,etc. JNU students are known for their strong analytical abilities, adaptability, and ethical outlook—qualities highly valued by employers. The Placement Cell is committed to offering a smooth and efficient recruitment experience, while building long-term, mutually beneficial relationships with recruiting partners.
